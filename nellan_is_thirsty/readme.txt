Nellan Is Thirsty
Is a text based puzzle game where you collect objects from different rooms and then
deposit the valuable Objects in the Bank, use other objects you collect to aide you to carry out certian 
tasks.
Your main aim is to get a bowl of cold milk to Nellan the giant cat, but its not as easy as it seems ;-)

Written by Dr Furman H Smith & Published in Recreational Computing magazine in 1980.
Ported shortly thereafter to the Commodore 64 by Steven Darnold.
Ported to BBC BASIC for the BBC Micro by lurkio in 2020 .
Ported to the Agon Light by Steve Lovejoy in 2024.
