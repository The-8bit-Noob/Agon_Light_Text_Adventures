 ***************************
 *       PLANET FALL       *
 ***************************

 A text adventure game in BBC Basic.
 Written in 1983 by Tim Garden & Ian Nicholls.
 Ported and enhanced for the Agon Light by Steve Lovejoy in 2024.

 # CD into PFALL folder.
 >*cd PFALL

 # Load PFALL1.bas.
 >LOAD"PFALL1.bas"
 >RUN

 Note1: PFALL2.bas is the main program, it's auto loaded from PFALL1.bas.
 Once you know the instructions you can just >LOAD"PFALL2.bas" to skip the intro and instructions.
