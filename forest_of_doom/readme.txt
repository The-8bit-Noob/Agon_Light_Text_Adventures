The Forest Of Doom

Written by S.W. LUCAS in 1983.
Ported to the BBC Micro in June 2020.
This port is from the ORIC version as printed in PCW magazine in Feb 1984,
but then slightly adjusted to bring it in line with the Commodore version.
Several improvements to the parser and bug fixes have been attempted.
Ported to the Agon Light By Steve Lovejoy 2024.
