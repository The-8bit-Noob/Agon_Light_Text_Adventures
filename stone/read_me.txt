 ***************************
 * THE PHILOSOPHER'S STONE *
 ***************************

 A text adventure game in BBC Basic.
 Written in 1986 by Graham J Barlow.
 Ported and enhanced for the Agon Light by Steve Lovejoy in 2024.

 # CD into stone folder.
 >*cd stone

 # Load stone1.bas.
 >LOAD"stone1.bas"
 >RUN

 Note1: stone2.bas is the main program, it's auto loaded from stone1.bas.
 Once you know the instructions you can just >LOAD"stone2.bas" to skip the intro and instructions.

 Note2: use the >SAVE command to save your progress in game.

 Enjoy....
